Pizza

(recipe for 2 portions)

Home

Ingredients:

    Dough

    • Flour 250g
    • Warm water 150ml
    • Dried yeasts 3-6g
    • Salt 0,5 of a teaspoon
    • Olive oil 1-2 spoons

    Toppings

    • Mozarella cheese (x2)
    • Pepperonni slices
    • Tamatoe paste
    • Basil

Method of preparation:

  1. Add flour, salt, olive oil to the bowl
  2. Add yeasts to the water and mix it
  3. Add water with yeasts to the bowl and mix it with the rest of the ingredients
  4. Leave dough under a bowl for arounf 45 minutes
